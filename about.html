About Finverse

Building a safer digital payment ecosystem

Our Mission

Finverse is dedicated to protecting users from UPI fraud and creating a safer digital payment environment. With the rapid growth of digital payments in India, fraudsters have become increasingly sophisticated in their attempts to steal money through fake UPI IDs and spoofed payment handles.

Our platform provides instant verification of UPI IDs, helping users identify suspicious payment handles before they become victims of fraud. By combining advanced fraud detection algorithms with community reporting, we create a comprehensive defense against UPI scams.

What We Detect

Fake Domains

We identify spoofed domains that mimic legitimate payment services like ok-sbi, support-paytm, gpay-care, and others designed to deceive users.

Invalid UPI Formats

Our system validates UPI ID formats and checks against known payment service provider domains to ensure authenticity.

Suspicious Patterns

We flag recently created handles and identify patterns commonly associated with fraudulent accounts.

Community Reports

We aggregate and analyze reports from users who have encountered suspicious UPI IDs, creating a community-driven safety network.

Safety Tips

  • Always verify UPI IDs before making payments, especially for large amounts
  • Be cautious of UPI IDs with unusual domains or spelling variations of known payment services
  • Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials with anyone
  • Report suspicious UPI IDs to help protect the community
  • Double-check recipient details before confirming any transaction
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links requesting UPI payments
  • Use official apps and websites for payment transactions
